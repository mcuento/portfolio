About Me

Hi! My name is Michael. I'm a Licensed Optician and currently work as a Clinical Operations Manager for Kaiser Permanente. I have a Master's degree in Health Administration from George Washington University and a business degree from George Mason University. I have no experience in IT. However, my passion for improving quality, cost, and the effectiveness of health care brought me here. The US currently spends more on healthcare than any other developed counties in the world. However, our results and overall population health are way behind the world leaders. There is a strong push in recent years to improve healthcare using technology. I want to use this experience to gain some skills to be a part of that transformation.
